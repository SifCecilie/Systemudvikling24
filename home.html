Welcome to MachuWay

Welcome to MachuWay, your guide to exploring the stunning landscapes and rich history of Machu Picchu. Our website is designed to help you plan your hiking adventure with ease. You’ll find general information about the history, weather, and local customs, as well as detailed maps and a customizable checklist to prepare for your journey. We offer a collection of tips and tricks to enhance your experience, along with a FAQ section to address common questions. Whether you’re looking for advice or insights, MachuWay is here to make your adventure unforgettable.
