MachuWay

Your ultimate guide for hiking in Machu Picchu

Information about this page

Welcome to MachuWay, your guide to exploring the stunning landscapes and rich history of Machu Picchu. Our website is designed to help you plan your hiking adventure with ease. You’ll find general information about the history, weather, and local customs, as well as detailed maps and a customizable checklist to prepare for your journey. We offer a collection of tips and tricks to enhance your experience, along with a FAQ section to address common questions. Whether you’re looking for advice or insights, MachuWay is here to make your adventure unforgettable.

General Information

Discover all the essential information you need to plan your hiking adventure in the stunning landscapes of Machu Picchu. Here you can find information about historical and cultural points of interest and learn about the best seasons to visit, local customs, and other useful details to make your planning experience as smooth as possible.

Tips and tricks

Look through our collection of tips and tricks that may better prepare you for the planning. Find tips about how to pack, good hiking advice and other useful tricks that might come in handy.

Checklist and map

Plan your hiking route with our interactive map of the hiking trails and surrounding area, to map out your hike and stay aware of nearby interesting locations. Here you will also find a nifty customizable checklist where you will be given some recommendations of what to bring for your trip.

FAQ

Here you can find and look trough all of our most frequently asked questions. This FAQ page has you covered with helpful insights and practical information to make your journey stress-free.
